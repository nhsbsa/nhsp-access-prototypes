What to do if you haven’t used NHS Login

This takes about 5 minutes to get set up. You’ll then be able to use it to access other NHS services.

What to do if your details change

You must tell your employer of any changes to your personal details. Incorrect details can impact and delay your NHS Pension.

If you have recently joined

We won't have any pension information from your employer yet, we get this information at the end of each financial year. You can find out more about your pension on the NHS Business Services Authority website.

If you sign up to our mailing list we’ll email you when your pension record is available to view.
